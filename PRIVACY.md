# Privacy Policy for TimeBlock

**Last Updated: January 11, 2026**

## Introduction

TimeBlock ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- **Email address**: Used for authentication and friend requests
- **Display name**: Shown to you and your friends on the leaderboard
- **Password**: Securely hashed and stored via Firebase Authentication

### 2. Usage Data
We collect and store:
- **Focus time data**: Total minutes you've focused
- **Timer sessions**: Duration and completion of focus sessions
- **Friend connections**: List of users you've added as friends
- **Friend requests**: Sent and received friend requests

### 3. Local Data
The extension stores locally on your device:
- **Timer state**: Current timer progress (survives browser restarts)
- **Daily statistics**: Today's focus time
- **User session**: Authentication token for staying logged in

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Uses
- **Timer Functionality**: Track and persist your focus sessions
- **Leaderboard**: Display your rank and compare with friends
- **Friend System**: Enable adding friends and viewing their progress
- **Notifications**: Alert you when focus sessions complete

### Data Sharing
- **With Friends**: Only users you explicitly add as friends can see:
  - Your display name
  - Your email address
  - Your total focus time
- **No Third Parties**: We do NOT sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers or third parties
- **Firebase**: Your data is stored on Google Firebase (see "Third-Party Services" below)

## Data Storage and Security

### Where Your Data is Stored
- **Firebase Authentication**: Securely stores your email and password hash
- **Cloud Firestore**: Stores your focus data, friends list, and friend requests
- **Chrome Local Storage**: Stores timer state and daily stats on your device

### Security Measures
- All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
- Passwords are hashed using Firebase's secure authentication
- Database access is protected by Firebase Security Rules
- Only authenticated users can access their own data

### Data Retention
- **Active Accounts**: Data is retained as long as your account exists
- **Account Deletion**: You can request account deletion by contacting us (see below)
- **Inactive Accounts**: We may delete accounts inactive for 2+ years

## Your Privacy Rights

### You Have the Right To:
1. **Access**: View all data we've collected about you
2. **Rectification**: Update your display name or email
3. **Deletion**: Request complete deletion of your account and data
4. **Portability**: Export your focus time data
5. **Opt-out**: Stop using the extension and delete your account at any time

## Permissions Explained

The extension requests these Chrome permissions:

### Required Permissions
- **storage**: Store timer state and preferences locally
- **notifications**: Alert you when focus sessions complete
- **alarms**: Keep timer running in background
- **identity**: (Future) For Google Sign-In option

### Host Permissions
- **firebase.googleapis.com**: Communicate with Firebase for authentication
- **firestore.googleapis.com**: Store and sync your focus data

We do NOT:
- Access your browsing history
- Read your emails or personal files
- Track your activity on other websites
- Access your camera, microphone, or location

## Third-Party Services

### Firebase by Google
We use Firebase for:
- **Authentication**: Secure login system
- **Firestore**: Cloud database for focus data
- **Privacy**: Firebase complies with GDPR and has its own privacy policy: [firebase.google.com/support/privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)

### No Analytics
We do NOT use:
- Google Analytics
- Third-party tracking pixels
- Advertising networks
- Usage analytics tools

## Children's Privacy

TimeBlock is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be:
- Posted on this page with a new "Last Updated" date
- Notified via the extension if changes are significant
- Effective immediately upon posting

## Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will:
1. Notify you via email within 72 hours
2. Explain what data was affected
3. Provide steps you can take to protect yourself

- Keeping you logged in
- Saving timer preferences
- Caching your daily stats
